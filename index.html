Don't Explode!

How to Play

  • Players take turns in tossing a bomb back and forth that starts on a count of 15.
  • Both players have the option of decreasing the counter by 1 or 2.
  • Try not to be the last with the bomb. Last player to count to 0 loses!
  • On rounds after the first, bomb count will vary from 15-20.
